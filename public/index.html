Tic Tac Toe

A game in which TWO players take turns placing an x or an o on a 3x3 grid, until a player has captured a line of 3 squares.

Dots and Boxes

A game in which TWO players take turns claiming lines to capture as many boxes as possible. If a player completes a box, they must claim another line.

Connect 4

A game in which TWO players compete to connect 4 tokens in a vertical, horizontal, or diagnal line in a 7x6 board
